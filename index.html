About Me

Who I Am

I am a 30 year old husband and father of two.

I graduated from St. Edward's University in 2009 with a BFA in Graphic Design and Codeup in 2017 with a focus on Full Stack + LAMP development.

A native Texan, I have lived in every major metropolitan area in the state, but now call San Antonio home.

I enjoy designing, coding, creating music, and spending time with my wonderful family.

What I Do

As a web developer and graphic designer I specialize in the development of user experiences on the Internet and assemble images, typography, and graphics in a way that is both accessible and memorable.

This enables me to create things such as, but not limited to:

  • Beautiful & responsive websites
  • Interactive web applications
  • Graphics for print & digital media
  • Logos & branding
  • Marketing materials
